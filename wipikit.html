some weight
lifts itself. what remains — you choose. the rest we carry.

quiet removal of what no longer fits. clothes, bags, shoes, small things — leaving the way they came: without ceremony.

within a day · no account
1

gather

what you no longer reach for. that’s all. no need to sort.

2

set at the threshold

the place where things arrive and depart — your building’s common room, the alcove by the mail, the step just inside.

leave it there.

one photo, and a few words on how to reach it.
we follow exactly what you leave for us.

3

we come by

if we receive your note before noon,
we pick up within a few hours.

after that,
within a day.

never late at night.

by nightfall,
it’s no longer yours to hold.

4

continue

what leaves you finds another — repaired if needed, held by someone new.
